Our Legal Practice Areas

We deal in the stated fields and have excellent experience in these fields ...

Intellectual Property Law

Intellectual property is a property right that relates to intangible property such as patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is the body of law that relates to crime. It proscribes conduct perceived as threatening, harmful, or otherwise endangering to the property, health, safety, and moral welfare of people.

Civil / M & A Law

The Civil Law consists of a body of rules, procedures, regulations and judicial precedents that helps in resolving the various non-criminal disputes. These disputes are either between individuals or organizations.

Corporate Law

Corporate law is related to commercial and contract law. A corporation is a legal entity created through the laws of its state of incorporation, treating a corporation as a legal "person" that has standing to sue and be sued, distinct from its stockholders.

Matrimonial Law

Within the field of family law are all kinds of issues such as marriage, separation, and divorce, as well as child adoption, custody, and access. Matrimonial law is a branch of family law that specifically deals with issues that relate to marriage.
